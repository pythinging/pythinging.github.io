Madeline is an 18 year old girl who suffered from an immuno-deficiency disease that prevented her from leaving her home. Her mother takes care of her with the help of a nurse named Carla, who took care of Maddy for 15 years. Everything changed when Maddy saw Olly through her window, and their story began. She had nothing to compare him to but soon he began to become her everything and together they had everthing to loose.

With an exploding romance budding between the two, will Maddie's mother be able to keep a scret she has hidden from her daughter for 18 years of her life? Will Olly be able to persue the passion he has develpoed for Maddie without hurting her? Will Maddie be able to love her mother so much that she pushes aside how she feels for Olly? Let the book take you on an expedition that will blow your mind with drama and romance.
